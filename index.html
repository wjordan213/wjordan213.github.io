About Me

I grew up in Greenwich CT, and attended Tulane University in New Orleans until I decided to leave to attend App Academy, a 12-week long developer boot camp. My interest in Computer Science began when I took an intro course 4 years ago.


From the moment I sat down with my first programming assignment until now, I've been in love with the process of encountering a new problem, dissecting it, and smashing my head against the keyboard until I press a button and see my work my pay off.


After a summer internship at the New York office of Konrad Group, a dev shop based out of Toronto, I realized that a career in software engineering would allow me to constantly learn more on the job, and I knew that was where I wanted to be.
